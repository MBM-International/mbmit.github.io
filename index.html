The Founders

The Company

MBM International was founded in 2009 by Mario Behling and DANG Hong Phuc. Since its inception the goal of the company is to lead international corporations and technology startups towards producing higher revenue generating products.

The company builds its operations on the basis of its internationally experienced staff and a proven track record of its founders. We enable our clients to provide better services and products. We are:

  • Offering our experience of over 10 years in the regions market with company's top management from Germany, Japan and Vietnam
