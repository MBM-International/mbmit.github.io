Hong Phuc Dang

After her childhood in Vietnam Hong Phuc lived several years in the US and Singapore. She is widely recognized as an Open Source community and graphics software expert. Mario Behling

Mario is an entrepreneur who grew up in Berlin, Germany. He is a Linux expert and the founder of the light Ubuntu distribution, Lubuntu. Mario has worked with the One Laptop per Child project in Afghanistan and blogs about Freifunk, the free wifi mesh project. Mario co-organized the enterprise matchmaking events FOSS Bridge in Asia.

The Company

MBM International was founded in 2009 by Mario Behling (Germany) and DANG Hong Phuc (Vietnam/Singapore) uniting independent operations of the partners. Marketing, ICT and International Services - MBM is leading the Vietnamese and Asian market towards innovation and producing higher revenue generating products. The registered limited company supports global clients online, in-house, at production facilities, at events, conferences and trade shows.

The company builds its operations on the basis of its internationally experienced staff and a proven track record of its founders in the business consultant, ICT and marketing sector.
