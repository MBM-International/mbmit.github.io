The Founders

Mario Behling is an entrepreneur who grew up in East-Berlin during the fall of the Berlin wall. He is a Linux expert and the founder of the light Ubuntu distribution, Lubuntu. Mario organized numerous enterprise matchmaking events like FOSS Bridge, deployed WiFi mesh networks in Afghanistan, and started the FOSSASIA community. He supports hardware projects like Crypto Stick, and engages with the Fashion and Tech community in Europe. Mario also build a hotel, set up a tour company in Vietnam and made a film in the Ukraine.

Hong Phuc Dang lived several years in the US and Singapore after her childhood in Vietnam. She is widely recognized as an Open Source community and graphics software expert. She has worked on research projects for top International data research companies. Hong Phuc has spoken at numerous International conferences and organized GNOME.Asia and FOSSASIA events.

The Company

MBM International was founded in 2009 by Mario Behling (Germany) and DANG Hong Phuc (Vietnam/Singapore). Since its inception the goal of the company is to lead international corporations and technology startups towards producing higher revenue generating products.

The company builds its operations on the basis of its internationally experienced staff and a proven track record of its founders. We enable our clients to provide better services and products.

  • Using latest technologies to solve real world problems
  • Leading business services with international consultants in Vietnam, China, Cambodia, Myanmar, and Singapore
  • Working efficiently across languages and cultural barriers
  • Offering our experience of over 10 years in the regions market of the company's top management from Germany, Japan and Vietnam
  • Providing direct access to top regional and national decision makers
  • Adhering to highest standards with management best practices, certified SCRUM Masters
  • Advancing customers to next level with distributed technologies, git, and tracking systems
