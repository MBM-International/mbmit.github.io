About

MBM International was founded in 2009 by Mario Behling (Germany) and DANG Hong Phuc (Vietnam/Singapore) uniting independent operations of the partners. Marketing, ICT and International Services - MBM is leading the Vietnamese and Asian market towards innovation and producing higher revenue generating products. The registered limited company offers its services throughout Vietnam and supports global clients online, in-house, at production facilities, at events, conferences and trade shows.

The company builds its operations on the basis of its internationally experienced staff and a proven track record of its founders in the business consultant, ICT and marketing sector.

The economic, security, reliability, and quality advantages of our services give organizations and enterprises a competitive advantage. We enable our clients to provide better services and products.
